Destination Highlights

Budapest, Hungary

I traveled there in August 2024.

The Best Things We Did:
  • Visit Buda Castle & Fisherman’s Bastion
  • Take a Danube River Cruise at Sunset
  • Walk Across the Chain Bridge
  • Try Hungarian Goulash and Local Cuisine

Paris, France

I traveled there in August 2024 during the Olympics.

The Best Things We Did:
  • Visit the Eiffel Tower & Trocadéro
  • Tour the Louvre Museum
  • Explore Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur
  • Walk along the Seine River
  • Enjoy Croissants & French Pastries

Tokyo, Japan

I traveled there in December 2024.

The Best Things We Did:
  • Explore Shibuya Crossing and Takeshita Street
  • Visit Meiji Shrine & Senso-ji Temple
  • Go to TeamLab Planets or Ghibli Museum
  • Take a Day Trip to Mt. Fuji (my wife's favorite)
  • Try Authentic Sushi and Ramen
